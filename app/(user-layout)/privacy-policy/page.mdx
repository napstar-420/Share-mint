# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 13th January 2025  
**Last Updated:** 13th January 2025  

This Privacy Policy outlines how **Sharemint** ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
### a. Personal Information
- Google Account details, including name, email address, and profile picture, when you log in using Google authentication.

### b. Uploaded Content
- Images you upload to our platform.
- Metadata associated with uploaded images (e.g., expiration settings, download limits, passwords).

### c. Automatically Collected Data
- Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage logs.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information for the following purposes:
- To provide and improve our services.
- To manage user accounts and allow access to uploaded images.
- To generate and manage shareable links for uploaded images.
- To enforce expiration settings, download limits, and password protections.
- To monitor and delete expired or limited-access images via cron jobs.
- To enhance security and prevent unauthorized access.

---

## 3. Sharing of Information
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:
- **With your consent:** When you share image links with others.
- **Service Providers:** For secure storage of uploaded images (e.g., Google Drive).
- **Legal Obligations:** To comply with applicable laws or enforce our rights.

---

## 4. Data Security
We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption for stored images and secure access protocols. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. User Rights
You have the following rights regarding your data:
- **Access:** View all your uploaded images and associated data through your account.
- **Delete:** Permanently delete any uploaded images at any time.
- **Modify Settings:** Update expiration dates, download limits, or password protections.

---

## 6. Cookies and Tracking
We use cookies to enhance your experience, such as maintaining your session. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our app is not intended for children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.
